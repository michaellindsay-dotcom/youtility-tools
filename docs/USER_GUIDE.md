# YoutilityKnock — User Guide

A field guide for reps and managers. YoutilityKnock helps door-to-door teams
map their turf, capture leads at the door, track shifts automatically, and pace
toward goals.

> **Getting an account:** there is no public sign-up. Your company admin creates
> your account and sends you an invite. If you can't log in, contact your admin.

---

## 1. Getting started

1. **Install the app** from the link your admin sends (App Store), then open it.
2. **Sign in** with the email + password your admin gave you. (On the web you can
   also use "Sign in with Google" if your admin enabled it.)
3. **Allow location** when prompted — the app uses it to show nearby homes and to
   confirm you're on-site when you knock. Location is used **only while the app is
   open**; there is no background tracking.

The header at the top shows your **company name**, a **notifications bell**, and
your **Start Shift / shift timer**. The menu (☰) opens the side navigation.

---

## 2. Working a shift

Your shift is what turns knocks into tracked stats.

- **Start a shift:** tap **▶ Start Shift** in the header. It turns into a live
  timer with your **door count**.
- **Knock doors:** open homes on the map and log them (next section). A knock
  counts toward your shift **only when you're within ~100 ft of the home** (the
  app checks GPS for you).
- **Stop a shift:** tap **Stop**. Shifts also **auto-stop after 5 minutes of
  inactivity**, so you won't accidentally leave one running.

---

## 3. The Map (your main screen)

Open **Map** from the menu. You'll see:

- **Home pins** around you — they load automatically as you move.
- **Leads** colored by their disposition.
- **Territories** your company has drawn, and recent **move-ins**.
- Map tools (top-left/right) to recenter, follow your location, switch
  satellite/street, and toggle layers.

**Tap any home** to open the homeowner card and log what happened.

---

## 4. Logging a door (the homeowner card)

The card opens in **two quick steps** so the first screen stays short.

**Step 1 — Disposition & contact**
1. Pick a **disposition** (see the list below).
2. Confirm/enter the **name, phone, email, and notes**. If we have data for the
   address, these may be pre-filled — just check them.
3. Tap the button at the bottom:
   - For **Appointment, Go Back, Pipeline, or Sold** → it reads **Next →** and
     takes you to step 2.
   - For everything else → it reads **Add Lead** and saves right away.

**Step 2 — Schedule & photos** (only for the dispositions above)
- **Schedule** the appointment / go-back / follow-up (a sensible time is
  pre-filled — adjust it). Appointments must fit your company's booking rules.
- **Add photos:** front of the home and/or the utility bill (optional).
- Tap **Add Lead** to save. Scheduled items show up on your **Schedule** screen.

Use **← Back** to return to step 1; **Cancel** or **✕** closes without saving.

**Dispositions**

| Disposition | When to use |
|-------------|-------------|
| New | Fresh contact, no outcome yet |
| Not Home 1 / Not Home 2 | Nobody answered (first / second attempt) |
| Go Back | Come back later — schedules a go-back |
| Pipeline | Interested, longer-term — schedules a follow-up |
| Appointment | Booked a sit-down — schedules the appointment |
| Not Interested | A clear no |
| Sold | Closed — add photos here |
| Do Not Contact | Never knock again |

> **On-site badge:** the card shows how far you are from the home. If you're too
> far (over ~100 ft), the lead still saves, but it won't count toward your door
> total or stats. Start a shift and be at the door for knocks to count.

## 5. Success Planner (your goals & pace)

Open **Success Planner** from the menu.

- **Set one number — your monthly close goal.** From it, the app works backward
  through **your own rolling 30-day averages** to show what it takes: closes,
  appointments, conversations, doors, and hours per **day, week, and month**.
- Those breakdown numbers are **calculated for you** — you don't edit them
  individually.
- **Your close goal can only be changed on Sundays.** Any other day it's locked
  (you'll see a 🔒 note) so your plan stays stable through the week.
- Doors and hours **adjust to your real pace from your first shift** (before
  that they use a starter baseline).
- **Your numbers:** the rolling averages that drive the math — you can fine-tune
  them to match your true rates.

At the bottom, **Shifts** shows **your last 30 days, one row per day** (shifts,
time, doors).

---

## 6. Staying motivated (if your plan includes it)

- **Dashboard** — today's funnel (doors → conversations → appointments →
  closes), how you're pacing to goal, and the week's top performers.
- **Leaderboard / Gamify / Rewards** — points, levels, badges, and rewards you
  unlock or redeem.
- **Team Chat / Who's Working** — message your team and see who's on shift right
  now; send a shout-out.

---

## 7. For managers

- **Team** — your downstream org chart (you see your reports and below; admins
  see the whole company).
- **Shift history (web console)** — the per-shift list for your downline, not
  just daily rollups.
- **Territories** — draw map territories and assign them to reps.
- **Schedule** — appointments and follow-ups across your team.

Visibility is enforced server-side: you only ever see your own downstream
activity.

---

## 8. Settings & help

- **Settings** — add a phone number (SMS fallback), link your Google/Microsoft
  calendar, or change your password.
- **Notifications** — the bell shows new chat messages, DMs, and calendar
  events.
- **Trouble logging in or missing a feature?** Contact your company admin — they
  manage accounts and which features your plan includes. For anything else,
  email **info@sunservice.io**.
