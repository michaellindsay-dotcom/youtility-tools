**Promotional text (≤170 chars)**
> The field app for door-to-door teams: map your turf, capture leads on the

**Description**
> YoutilityKnock is the field companion for door-to-door sales and canvassing
> teams. Built for reps who work neighborhoods all day, it puts your territory,
> your leads, and your numbers in one fast, offline-friendly app.
>
> • Map your turf — see homes around you, drop pins, and confirm a knock right
>   on the doorstep with on-site location.
> • Capture leads in seconds — record the homeowner, the disposition, and notes
>   without losing momentum. Attach a photo of the home or a utility bill.
> • Work your territories and shifts — know where you've been, what's left, and
>   who's on your team.
> • Stay in sync — leads and activity sync to your company automatically, and
>   queued work survives a dead zone thanks to offline support.
> • Built-in hierarchy — managers see their team's activity; reps see their own.
>
> YoutilityKnock accounts are provisioned by your company administrator. If you
> don't have a login yet, contact your admin.
>
> Location is used only while the app is open, to show nearby homes and confirm
> on-site knocks — there is no background tracking.
